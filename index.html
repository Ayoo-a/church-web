Love

We teach men to see and know the love of God. This we do by teaching men how to love their neighbour. Loving our neighbour is beyond not keeping malice, but by preaching the Gospel to them and being an example.

Knowledge

We teach men the accurate and precise knowledge of Christ so that they can walk in such knowledge, not in arrogance but in liberty of the finished work of Christ, while being able to teach others

Fellowship

We teach men how to fellowship with God continually through intimacy, prayers, the fellowship of the Saints and the gathering of believers.
